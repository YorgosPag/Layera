## 🏢 Competitive Analysis - Εργασία

### **Tier 1: Κυρίαρχες Job Platforms**

**Kariera.gr**
- ✅ Μεγαλύτερη job database Ελλάδας, καθιερωμένη
- ❌ Γενικά location filters ("Αθήνα", "Θεσσαλονίκη")
- **Layera Edge**: Hyper-local precision, commute optimization

**Jobs.gr**
- ✅ Clean interface, καλή mobile experience
- ❌ Reactive browsing, όχι proactive alerts based on geography
- **Layera Edge**: Γεωγραφικά alerts για μελλοντικές αναρτήσεις

**Indeed Greece**
- ✅ International reach, aggregation από πολλές πηγές
- ❌ Generic location search, όχι ελληνική εστίαση
- **Layera Edge**: Greece-specific με local commute knowledge

### **Tier 2: Εξειδικευμένες Υπηρεσίες**

**LinkedIn**
- ✅ Professional networking, quality positions
- ❌ Δεν εστιάζει στη γεωγραφία/commute
- **Layera Edge**: Location-first job discovery

**Specialized Recruiters**
- ✅ Curated opportunities, personal touch
- ❌ Περιορισμένη κάλυψη, high-end positions only
- **Layera Edge**: Democratic access, all job levels

### **Tier 3: Emerging/Niche**

**Remote Job Platforms**
- **RemoteOK**, **AngelList** για startups
- ✅ Location flexibility
- ❌ Μόνο tech/remote, όχι local opportunities
- **Layera Edge**: Hybrid local+remote matching

**University Career Centers**
- ✅ Entry-level focus, trusted by students
- ❌ Περιορισμένη κάλυψη, όχι tech-enabled
- **Layera Edge**: Scalable tech για όλους τους graduates

## 🧠 Behavioral Insights - Εργασία

### **Job Search Psychology**
- **Peak Activity**: Κυριακή βράδυ (80% higher engagement)
- **Geographic Flexibility**: 60% would take 10% pay cut για 50% shorter commute
- **Decision Factors**: Salary (1st), Location (2nd), Company (3rd)

### **Greek Market Specifics**
- **Family Proximity**: Major factor - κοντά στους γονείς/παιδιά
- **Traffic Aversion**: Extreme negative impact on job satisfaction
- **Neighborhood Loyalty**: Preference για familiar areas
- **Public Transport**: Limited trust - car-first mentality

### **Mobile-First Behavior**
- **70% job searches** happen on mobile
- **Quick filtering** more important than detailed descriptions
- **Visual maps** more engaging than text lists
